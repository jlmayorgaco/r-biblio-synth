\section*{PRISMA 2020 Methodology}
Methodological mode: full-review.
Records were identified from database sources (n = 13020) and other sources (n = 0). 
Deduplication removed 1189 records using doi\_exact; title\_year\_normalized.
Screening considered 11831 records; 9288 were excluded at title/abstract level. 
Eligibility assessment reviewed 2543 full-text records and excluded 775. 
The final synthesis included 1768 studies.
 The primary database was scopus and the main search string was TITLE-ABS-KEY(("power system*" OR grid OR microgrid) AND ("frequency estimation" OR "frequency estimator")).
 The search covered 1970 to 2026, restricted to English and document types article, review, conference paper.
 Explicit screening decisions were recorded for 11831 documents.
 Reviewer agreement yielded a mean Cohen's $\kappa$ of 0.657 and Fleiss' $\kappa$ of -0.803, with Krippendorff's $\alpha$ of 0.943.
